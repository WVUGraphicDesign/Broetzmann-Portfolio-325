This was an infographic I created for Customer Care
Measurement & Consulting to display the results of a
customer delight survey the company had done recently.
The design is bright and poppy to accompany the results.
Sadly, I no longer have the sketches for this project, as
they were thrown away by mistake during a cleaning day.

The orange and black in the color palette was done at the suggestion of my boss to make the infographic look more visually active and less repetitive than the original palette of just blue and green. No mockups exist for this infographic.
